Quick Bite #1

"Default to AI First"

Habit
🎯 Action
Before starting any task (email, meeting, decision), ask AI to help think it through first.
💡 Why It Matters
Rewires your instinct from "I'll do it myself" → "AI helps me think first." Compounds time savings and clarity.
🎨 Creative Twist
Try it on a personal task — planning a weekend, phrasing a kind message to a friend.
📝 Team-Shareable Insight
"Remember: AI first, action second."

# 🧠 Kairo Bites — AI Quick Bite Generator

Daily AI training tips to build your AI muscle memory, one bite at a time.

## Features

- **🎰 Random Bite Generator** — Get fresh AI tips from a curated library
- **🔥 Streak Tracker** — Track your daily consistency
- **🎯 Mode Filters** — Work, Creative, Personal, or Any
- **📋 One-Click Copy** — Share tips with your team instantly
- **📚 Recent History** — Revisit previous bites

## Quick Bites Include

- "Default to AI First" — Build the habit
- "The 80/20 Prompt" — Refine, don't restart
- "AI Meeting Prep" — 5-minute prep hack
- "The Devil's Advocate" — Stress-test decisions
- "Morning Brain Dump" — Clear mental clutter
- And more...

## Getting Started

Just open `index.html` in a browser, or deploy to Vercel for a live URL.
